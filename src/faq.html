Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our luxury fashion

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

We process orders within 24-48 hours. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within India. International shipping may take 7-14 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached.

How do I exchange an item?

Contact our customer service team to initiate an exchange. We'll guide you through the process and help find the right size or style.

Product & Care

How should I care for my garments?

Each garment comes with specific care instructions. Generally, we recommend dry cleaning for delicate pieces and hand washing for others.

Are your sizes standard?

We follow standard Indian sizing. Please refer to our detailed size guide for measurements to ensure the perfect fit.
