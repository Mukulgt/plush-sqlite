Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days) and express shipping (1-2 business days) across India. Free shipping is available on orders above ₹2999.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 15-day return policy for all unused items in their original packaging. Returns are free, and refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website or the courier's website.
