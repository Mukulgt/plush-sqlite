Size Guide

Find your perfect fit with our detailed size guide and measurement instructions.

How to Measure

Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape parallel to the ground.

Measure around your natural waistline, keeping the tape comfortably loose.

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 8 inches below your waist.

Measure from the top of your inner thigh to your ankle.

Fit Tips

  • If you're between sizes, order the larger size for a more comfortable fit.
  • Use a flexible tape measure and measure against your body, not over clothes.
  • For the most accurate measurements, have someone else measure you.
  • Check individual product descriptions for specific fit recommendations.
